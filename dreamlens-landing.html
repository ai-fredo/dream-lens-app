Questions

Common questions

Is my dream content private? +

Yes. Your dream transcripts are stored encrypted and are never shared with third parties or used for advertising. Interpretation uses a curated local knowledge base — not a public web search — so your content stays within DreamLens.

How accurate is the voice transcription? +

DreamLens uses Whisper (OpenAI's speech recognition model), which achieves over 90% accuracy on clear speech. You can review and edit the transcription before it's submitted for interpretation.

How does the memory/pattern system work? +

Each dream entry is analyzed for symbols, themes, and emotional tone. These are tracked over time. When a symbol or theme appears three or more times, DreamLens begins noting it in your interpretations. After 30+ entries, a full Dream Profile is available showing your subconscious patterns in aggregate.

Is this a mental health app? +

No. DreamLens is a journaling and reflection tool grounded in psychological frameworks. It is not therapy, does not provide medical advice, and is not a substitute for professional mental health support. If you're experiencing distress, please reach out to a qualified professional.

What does it cost? +

DreamLens will launch with a free tier (10 dreams with full interpretation) and a Pro plan (~$7.99/month or $59.99/year) that includes unlimited entries, pattern analysis, search, and your full Dream Profile. Early waitlist members will receive a founding discount.
